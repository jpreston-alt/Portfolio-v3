About Me

Hi! I'm Joanna, welcome to my site. I’m a designer, builder, and full-stack web developer. I spent the last five years of my career in retail design working for Urban Outfitters as a Market Display Artist. My career in design has taught me how to learn quickly, creatively solve problems, and build things that are both functional and visually engaging. It's my passion for making things that made me fall in love with web development ever since my very first "Hello World".

Currently, I'm finishing up a Certificate in Full-Stack Web Development from the University of Washington and am working hard to transition into web development as a career. I am excited about merging my background in design with my newly developed technical skills, to build both useful and beautiful things on the web.
